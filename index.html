Final Project Proposal

Trump vs. Putin vs. the World

Description

This project aims to illustrate the public perceptions of Russia and the United States by way of their leaders. My original idea for this project was to track public sentiment of Russia since the Cold War to see how perceptions may have changed since, and especially in the light of recent political events. While investigating potential data for this project, I realized that it’s hard to track sentiment data across time, but there is quite a bit of global data regarding country’s perceptions and faith in Trump and Putin. This visualization project would aim to showcase the different country’s opinions on the two different leaders across different topics such as confidence in Putin as a leader, confidence in the different governments, and more.
